The logarithmic integral function

The logarithmic integral function of J. P. G. L. Dirichlet is an improvement of Gauss' first estimation of the number of primes, the prime number theorem.
Like the prime number theorem, the logarithmic integral gives an approximation of the number of prime numbers up to a given natural number n. For big values of n, the function converges to the actual number of prime numbers π(n).
